About Eiichiro Oda

Eiichiro Oda, born on January 1, 1975, in Kumamoto, Japan, is a renowned manga artist best known for creating the immensely popular series One Piece1 . Since its debut in 1997, One Piece has become the best-selling manga in history, with over 523 million copies in circulation worldwide

Achievements

Eiichiro Oda's achievements are monumental in the world of manga. He created the legendary series One Piece, which has smashed records as the best-selling manga of all time, with over 523 million copies sold worldwide. His work has won prestigious awards, including the Grand Prize at the 41st Japan Cartoonists Association Award, and has influenced countless creators and storytellers. Oda's dedication to his craft is evident in his meticulous storytelling and world-building, and his ability to captivate audiences for over two decades has cemented his place as one of the most influential figures in manga history.
